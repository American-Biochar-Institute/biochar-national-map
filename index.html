About this map

Each color is the USDA-NRCS interpretation SOH - Dynamic Soil Properties Response to Biochar, which estimates how much biochar is expected to improve a soil's dynamic properties. Higher means more expected gain. Draw-an-area detail is handled by the Institute's separate area tool.

The rating describes the soil as mapped, in its native condition, not a drained or managed field. Areas with no SSURGO rating are left unshaded.
